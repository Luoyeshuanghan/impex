x-style支持不同的写法：
  • <div x-style="{'font-size': valExp}">...</div>
  • <div x-style="{'fontSize': valExp}">...</div>
  • <div x-style="obj">...</div>

demo 1

<div x-style=" {'color':color,'font-size':size}"> style </div>
style
color: size:

demo 2

<div x-style=" style"> style </div>
style
style.color: style.fontSize:
